Urban space and users

Bachelor research on the impact of graphic design on our relationship with spaces

With this topic, I examine the importance of images in our daily lives : « In a society where our environments are saturated and our minds overstimulated, can graphic design play a remedial role in our relationship with spaces ? » I address both the issue of attention and the way we move through space. Various installations and opinion polls fuel researches.
