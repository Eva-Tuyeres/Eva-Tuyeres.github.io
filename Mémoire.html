Fragmented love

Experimental edition

Experimentation aimed at breaking publishing rules through new uses of InDesign tools. With all my classmates, we published a new version of Roland Barthes’ Fragments d’un discours amoureux. To enhance the reading experience and offer something new, the 80 themes have been graphically transposed through the layout and typography.
