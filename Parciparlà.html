Log Off

Marketing campaign

Log Off is a movement that rethinks our digital practices. Since the target audience is Auguste Renoir’s students, the campaign’s identity should not cause anxiety about this serious subject. The identity developed with Camille Orieux uses a user metaphor : the fish for a quirky
and lighthearted approach. A final booklet compiles the students’ thoughts on the role of digital technology in their daily lives.
