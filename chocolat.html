Marketing campaign

New campaign for the opening of new stores in the Guerrisol second-hand store chain. Guerrisol wants to emphasize that dressing up is a fun and creative activity. The drawn shapes transform the silhouette into an abstract, off-the-wall and uninhibited composition. I experienced the idea of creativity using different techniques (painting, photography, ...)
