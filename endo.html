Endo

Fanzine

Micro-publication dealing with endometriosis: a chronic disease that affects 1 in 10 women. The typography of the title aims to raise awareness of this disease, with no name on the cover, only key figures that arouse curiosity. This publication aims to raise awareness of the debilitating effects of this disease on everyday life.
