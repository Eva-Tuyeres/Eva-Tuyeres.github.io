D’art D’art festival

Event graphic identity

Design of a poster, program, and animation for « D’art D’art », a street performance festival organized by the Happy Culture association. The colourful 3D shapes are inspired by the urban decorations that adorn the city during the event. The identity exudes a festive, family-friendly, and lighthearted atmosphere thanks to its shapes and patterns.
