The glass half full

Newspaper

Graphic identity for a weekly newspaper : The glass half full For the Média Positif. This newspaper only publishes good news. To highlight this new outlook on news, I propose two different layouts, in blue and red. We can read the news thanks to two colored transparent papers. This newspaper offers a playful scenario, bringing back memories of our childhood.
